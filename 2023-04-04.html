It is definitely not a room I would recommend to those who enjoy “sunshine” coming through! We asked for a change (and were prepared to pay for the additional charge), but were simply denied the option. This was a little bit of a surprise as the hotel was not particularly busy during the period. Surprisingly – and this may be the exception that confirms the rule -, the staff at the reception was not particularly welcoming either. They barely answered the phone and did not show the degree of customer care that we are now used to in this beautiful country. Did we visit on a bad day or at a bad time? This experience was a real disappointment and we will not come back to this hotel when visiting Bangkok again.
